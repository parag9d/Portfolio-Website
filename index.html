Pickles - Mobile

Location based food recommendation app.

Being a foodie myself I always feel the urge to try new cuisines and taste the regional delicacies whenever I visit a new place. This brings me to the hard part of finding the foods that are typical of those places or are recommended for any city. I searched through the plethora of apps but it made me realise that overtime the focus has shifted from the food to other experiences built around it. But they don’t essentially solve my problem of knowing what to try when coming to a new city.

So, I designed “Pickles” with the main focus on the food. It gives a curated list of recommended food based on your location, so you no longer have to miss out on those lip smacking delicacies whenever you visit a new place. It also gives you recommended restaurants for the food you pick and makes the process so much simpler.
